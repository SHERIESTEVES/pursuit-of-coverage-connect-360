Platform vision

Designed around the real CalAIM workflow, not generic medical billing.

CalAIM Connect360 is a full-service practice management platform for organizations managing Enhanced Care Management, Community Supports, Housing Intervention, pre-release and post-release services, vendor-funded supports, and multi-party care coordination.

It connects members, providers, care managers, vendors, facilities, criminal justice partners, public agencies, and billing teams through a permission-based shared care record.

Core modules

Everything needed to run ECM, CS, HI, and FFS services from intake to payment.

Member & Care Team Hub

Unified member profile, care team directory, shared care plan, consent management, secure messaging, and portal access.

EHR & Documentation

Structured assessments, progress notes, care plans, service templates, electronic signatures, version history, and audit-ready records.

Appointments & Dispatch

Calendar scheduling, recurring visits, escort requests, transportation coordination, reminders, check-in, attendance, and service confirmation.

Authorization Management

Track authorizations, units, tiers, service dates, expiration alerts, authorization documents, and payer-specific billing rules.

Claims, EDI & AR

Electronic eligibility, CMS-1500, 837P/837I, 999, 277CA, 835 ERA posting, denial queue, PDR, corrected claims, and appeal workflows.

Vendor & Accounting Portal

Vendor onboarding, W-9 upload, check requests, invoices, purchase order linkage, proof of service, GL coding, and AP review.

Capacity Tracking

Caseload limits, acuity tiers, staff availability, vendor capacity, facility bed availability, and assignment recommendations.

Reporting & Compliance

DHCS, MCP, quality, utilization, encounter, claims, capacity, outreach, enrollment, and audit readiness dashboards.

Workflow

From referral to reimbursement in one connected path.

1

Referral & Eligibility

Receive referral, verify Medi-Cal or MCP coverage, capture consent, and assign program pathway.

2

Assessment & Care Plan

Complete required templates, define goals, obtain e-signatures, and link the care team.

3

Services & Vendors

Schedule visits, dispatch escorts, request checks, confirm attendance, and collect proof of service.

4

Claims & Accounting

Generate charges, submit EDI claims, process invoices, post payments, and manage denials.

5

Reports & QA

Produce DHCS/MCP reports, capacity dashboards, quality metrics, and audit packets.

Security & compliance

Built for sensitive care coordination.

Role-based access: Separate permissions for care managers, billing, vendors, members, facilities, APS, parole/probation, and external providers.

Consent controls: Member-specific releases, expiration dates, sharing limits, and behavioral health/SUD privacy protections.

Audit trail: Every view, edit, upload, signature, claim action, and portal message is timestamped.

Data exchange: FHIR/HL7, EDI, HIE/ADT feeds, secure messaging, document exchange, and accounting exports.
